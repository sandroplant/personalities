Data Deletion Policy for Personalities

Last Updated: [Date]

At Personalities, we respect your right to have your personal data deleted. You can request the deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

Steps to Delete Your Data

  1. Log in to your account on www.personalities.life.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings page.
  3. Select Delete My Account and confirm your request.
  4. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

If you prefer, you may send a request to sandroplant@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and provide your account email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Data Deletion policy, please contact us at sandroplant@gmail.com.
